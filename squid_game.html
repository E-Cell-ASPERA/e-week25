Frequently Asked Questions

Is this event based on the Netflix series?
While inspired by the popular series, our event is a completely safe and fun competition. There are no real dangers or eliminations - just exciting challenges designed to test your mental skills and strategic thinking.
How many participants can be in a team?
This is a SOLO event, there are no teams in here... For obvious reasons. 😈
Do I need special skills to participate?
No special skills are required! The event tests general knowledge, strategic thinking, and basic game skills that anyone can participate in. What you will need is a quick mind, nerves of steel, and the ability to make tough decisions under pressure.
What should we wear for the event?
Participants will not be provided with numbered tracksuits inspired by the show. Please wear any casual-wear and attend.
Are there prizes for the winners?
Absolutely! The winning team will receive a substantial cash prize, exclusive merchandise, and championship trophies. Runner-up teams will also receive recognition and prizes. The exact prize pool will be announced at the opening ceremony.
