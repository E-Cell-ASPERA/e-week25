Event Overview

Welcome to IDEA TO ENTERPRISE 2025, where participants will embark on an entrepreneurial journey from concept to creation. In this immersive business simulation, you'll establish and manage virtual companies, navigate financial challenges, pitch to investors, and compete to build the most successful enterprise.

This dynamic event provides a realistic platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to learn business management through real-world simulations. You'll handle finances, investments, and operational strategies while adapting to market fluctuations as you compete for the highest company valuation.

Event Objectives

Business Management Through Simulation

Learn essential business management skills through immersive real-world simulations that mirror the challenges and decisions entrepreneurs face daily.

Financial & Operational Strategy

Develop expertise in handling finances, securing investments, and implementing operational strategies that drive business growth and success.

Market Adaptation & Competition

Learn to adapt to market fluctuations and business challenges while competing with other participants to achieve the highest company valuation.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior business experience to participate?
No prior business experience is required. IDEA TO ENTERPRISE 2025 is designed for participants at all levels, from beginners to those with some business knowledge. We provide the necessary resources and guidance to help you succeed in the competition.
How many members can be in a team?
Teams can consist of 2-4 members. This size allows for diverse perspectives and skills while ensuring every team member has a meaningful role in developing and running the virtual company.
What resources will be provided during the event?
Participants will receive access to business simulation experience, market data, mentorship sessions with industry experts, worksheets for financial planning, and guidelines for each stage of the competition. Additional resources will be available to help teams navigate challenges.
Goodies will be provided , laptops are mandatory
How will our virtual companies be evaluated?
Evaluation criteria include company valuation, profitability, market share, innovation, sustainability, team collaboration, presentation quality, and the ability to adapt to unexpected market changes. A panel of industry experts will judge the final presentations.
Are there any prizes for winning teams?
Yes! Winning teams will receive recognition certificates, trophies, and prizes including potential internship opportunities, mentorship sessions with successful entrepreneurs, and resources to help translate their virtual company into a real-world startup.
