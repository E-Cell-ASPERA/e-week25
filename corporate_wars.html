Event Overview

Welcome to the Corporate Wars! In this competition, you and your team will navigate through four rounds, each designed to test your strategic skills and business acumen. This immersive 3-day business simulation will challenge your decision-making abilities as you bid on assets, make strategic investments, negotiate sales, and present your financial outcomes.

Corporate Wars provides a realistic business environment where participants can apply theoretical knowledge to practical scenarios, enhancing their understanding of market dynamics, financial management, and strategic planning.

Competition Rounds

Round 1: Auction Round

Objective: To secure an island through bidding.

In this round, participants will engage in a bidding process to acquire an island. Each team will be provided with a set amount of purchase power to participate in the auction. All teams will successfully acquire an island during the bidding process. Once the auction round concludes, the teams proceed to the next round.

Round 2: Investing Round

Objective: To strategically invest in assets and resources.

In the investing round, teams will utilize the purchase power allocated to them during the auction round to invest in additional assets and resources to enhance their island. Teams can purchase add-ons such as infrastructure, amenities, or utilities to improve the value of their island. Additionally, teams have the option to pitch their ideas to investors for additional funding in exchange for equity.

Round 3: Distribution Round

Objective: To generate profit through sales.

In the distribution round, teams must sell the assets separately and resources acquired in the previous round to generate revenue. This round tests teams' sales and negotiation skills as they strive to maximize profits. The team that generates the highest profit will have highest score.

Round 4: Presentation Round

Objective: To showcase strategies, outcomes, and financial performance.

In the presentation round, teams will create a comprehensive presentation detailing their journey from the auction round to the distribution round. Teams must showcase detailed calculations of their purchases and sales, including the breakdown of expenses and profit. The team that achieves the break-even point faster and accumulates the highest profit within the timeline will get add on points.

Frequently Asked Questions

What skills will I develop during Corporate Wars?
Corporate Wars is designed to enhance your strategic thinking, negotiation skills, financial analysis, presentation abilities, teamwork, and decision-making under pressure. You'll gain practical experience in business operations from bidding to profit generation.
How many members can be in a team?
Each team should consist of 3-4 members. This size allows for diverse perspectives while ensuring every member has a meaningful role to play in the competition.
Do I need prior business knowledge to participate?
No, Corporate Wars is designed to be accessible to participants with various levels of business knowledge. Basic understanding of business concepts is helpful but not required. Our pre-event materials will help you prepare for the competition.
What should we prepare for the presentation round?
Your presentation should include your bidding strategy, investment decisions, sales tactics, and overall outcomes. The exact details will be announced at the beginning of the event.
Are there any prizes for winning teams?
Yes, the winning teams will receive recognition certificates, trophies, and prizes. The exact prize details will be announced at the beginning of the event. Beyond material prizes, participants gain valuable hands-on business experience that enhances their resume and professional skills.
